MeshSense provides a unifying orchestration layer for partner integrations spanning MNOs, banks, and remittance networks.

## What this product area includes

- Partner onboarding and connectivity patterns
- Orchestration across external channels and providers
- Event handling for multi-party workflows
- Operational guidance for network integrations
